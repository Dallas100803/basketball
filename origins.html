Origins of Basketball

Basketball is a popular sport played worldwide, and its origins can be traced back to the late 19th century.

James Naismith and the Invention of Basketball

In December 1891, Canadian physical education instructor James Naismith was tasked with creating a new game to keep his students active during the winter months. The result was basketball, a game initially played with a soccer ball and two peach baskets used as goals.

Spread and Evolution of the Game

From its humble beginnings, basketball quickly gained popularity and spread to other parts of the United States and beyond. The first official game was played at the International YMCA Training School in Springfield, Massachusetts.
